Rules

These are the rules for the contest & how to enter

  • File Formats

    PNG or JPG

    The only file formats excepted for the contest are either PNG or JPG. No PSDs, TIFFs or RAW photos.

  • Size Limit

    8MB

    All images submitted for the contest must be 8MB or less. Anything over the file size limit will automatically be disqualified.

  • Categories

    Landscape, Portrait, Urban

    All photos must fall under one of these three categories: Landscape, Portrait or Urban.

  • Deadlines

    3 Days Before End Of Month

    All photos submitted must be at least 3 days before the end of the month. The last 3 days are when the selection and voting process takes place.

  • Submit your photo today!
